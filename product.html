Basic Information On Plavix

This drug is used to prevent the coagulation of blood in platelets. These clots are generally caused by certain blood vessel or heart conditions. The Plavix drug is also used for the purpose of reducing the risk of blood clotting after heart attack or stroke. However, this drug makes it easier for you to bleed. This can cause heavy bleeding in case of minor injuries. You may also suffer from internal bleeding after using this drug. If you experience bloody or black stool, inform your doctor immediately. This may be a sign of stomach or intestinal bleeding. This risk is further aggravated by alcohol; therefore avoid drinking alcohol while you are on this drug.

Precautions / Side Effects For Plavix

Clopidogrel is the main ingredient of this drug. You should not take this medicine if you are allergic to this substance. If you need to undergo any surgery, you should inform your doctor about your Plavix use. Similar precaution should be taken if you decide to go for dental work. Generic or brand Plavix should not be used if you have suffered from stomach ulcers. You may need to undertake special tests before taking this medicine, if you ever had any blood clotting disorder.

If you have suffered from stroke or mini stroke, you should inform your doctor. Generic or brand Plavix is not known to cause any harm to an unborn baby, however you should still inform your health care provider if you get pregnant. It is not known whether the drug passes into breast milk or not. Therefore, you should not take Clopidogrel if you are nursing a baby.

Plavix Dosage

The initial recommended dosage of the drug is 75 mg once daily. The medicine can be taken with or without food. However, you should take this medicine according to your doctor’s advice. This medicine is available in two strengths of 75 mg and 300 mg. The 75 mg tablet is pink in color and is round in shape, whereas 300 mg drug comes in the form of an oblong, film-coated pink tablet. Plavix should be carefully stored. The ideal temperature for storing this drug is between 15 degree and 30 degree Celsius.
